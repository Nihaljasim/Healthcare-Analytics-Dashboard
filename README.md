# Healthcare-Analytics-Dashboard
End-to-end Healthcare analytics project using MySQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau

## Project Overview

-An end-to-end healthcare analytics project built using MySQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to analyze patient trends, doctor workload, clinical risk, and treatment costs.

-The project involves data modeling and KPI creation using SQL, followed by interactive dashboards with slicers and drill-downs to support resource planning, cost optimization, and decision-making.

-Key insights include workload imbalance across departments, high-risk patient segments, and cost variations by age group and diagnosis.

## Tools & Technologies

-MySQL – Data modeling, SQL views, KPI analysis

-Excel – Data validation and exploratory analysis

-Power BI – Interactive dashboards and KPIs

-Tableau – Advanced visual analytics and storytelling

## KPI Snapshot 

-10K Total Patients indicate a high-volume healthcare facility.

-1K Doctors shows a wide provider base supporting patient demand.

-Avg Treatment Cost ₹524.7 reflects controlled per-visit spending.

-33.5% Abnormal Lab Results signals moderate clinical risk requiring attention.

-Avg Patient Age 49 years suggests a predominantly adult and middle-aged population.

-₹25.4M Total Treatment Cost highlights significant operational expenditure.

## Dashboard Pages & Insights
###  Healthcare Summary

-The hospital handles high patient volume with controlled average treatment costs.

-33.5% abnormal lab results indicate notable clinical risk.

-General Medicine and Pediatrics show the highest doctor workload.

-Adult and senior patients dominate visits, highlighting chronic care demand.

-Monthly visit trends show seasonal fluctuations requiring staffing flexibility.

###  Healthcare Overview

-Months with higher visits also show higher follow-up rates, indicating increased treatment complexity.

-Senior patients consistently drive higher treatment costs across departments.

-Workload distribution reveals doctor imbalance, with few doctors handling most visits.

-Diagnosis analysis highlights lifestyle-related conditions such as diabetes and hypertension.

-Cost and workload heatmaps help identify high-pressure departments.

## Business Impact

-Identified high-risk departments based on abnormal lab trends

-Highlighted doctor workload imbalance for better resource allocation

-Enabled cost control analysis by age group and department

-Supported preventive care planning using diagnosis and age trends
